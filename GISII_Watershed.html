Watershed Delineation

Watersheds are areas that show where water drains. If rain were to land inside a watershed all the water would flow to the same river/lake/sea. The process to delineate watersheds in GIS is a fairly simple one and I use it later on in this class for my final project that looks at the Minnesota River watershed.
