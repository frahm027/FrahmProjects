A Story Map is a great tool to display spatial data in the form of a linear story and for my project I chose to show the history of the French Indian War. The story map goes through each important battle and what the troop movements were. Some troop movements are more accurate than others depending on what information I could gather on where the armies went.

 

If you would to have a better view of the story map you can find it on ESRI's site. French Indian War
